# Happy Pets - Kid-Friendly Pet Care App

## Vision

**"Caring is more important than earning."**

A gentle responsibility builder for kids ages 5–12. Not a game — a tool that teaches empathy and care for pets without pressure, streaks, or competition.

---

## Core Principles

- No streak pressure
- No punishment
- No competition
- No addiction loops
- Just: "You helped a living being today."

## Feature Details

### Home Screen
- Soft illustration or photo card per pet
- Tap pet → opens care page

### Pet Profile
- Name, type, photo/icon
- Birthday / adoption day
- Fun facts, favorite treats
- Vet notes (parent-only)

### Daily Care Tasks
| Task | Example |
|------|---------|
| Feed | Morning / Evening |
| Water | Check bowl |
| Walk | Dogs |
| Clean | Cage / litter |
| Brush | Fur |
| Play | 10 minutes |
| Check | Eyes / ears |

Big friendly buttons. Tap = done. No timers.

### Reward System
- Hearts, leaves, stars, paw prints
- Fill a kindness jar or grow a garden
- Visual growth only — no numbers

### Badges (Optional)
- "Kind Helper"
- "Gentle Brusher"
- "Water Watcher"
- "Clean Cage Champion"
- "Playtime Hero"

No ranking.

## Why This App Is Different

Most pet apps track data and show charts — they feel like chores.

This app builds **emotional responsibility** through:
- Gentle visual rewards
- Memory-building features
- No pressure mechanics
- Family connection
